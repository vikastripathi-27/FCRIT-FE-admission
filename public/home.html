Admission Guidelines

  1. Institute Level admissions (20% of the sanctioned intake) are done as per the guidelines of the DTE / Admission Regulating Authority /Government of Maharashtra.
  2. The aspiring eligible candidate must separately apply to the Institute for admission under Institute Level quota.
  3. All admissions under this quota will be on the basis of inter-se merit of the candidates applying to this Institute.
  4. Candidates can make an online application for the desired course, upload the required documents (HSC, MH-CET, JEE, SSC, LC) and submit after paying the processing fees of Rs.1000/-.
  5. The online application has to be submitted within the stipulated time.
  6. A Separate merit list will be prepared and the same will be displayed on the Institutes website under Admission Tab and also at the Institute Notice Board.
  7. Candidates are required to visit the Institute website from time to time for further updates.
